The Heritage Commuter

This bike brings together the charm of a classic design with the performance and comfort expected from a modern ride. Clean lines and timeless styling are paired with contemporary components to create a bike that feels familiar yet fresh.

Designed for everyday use, it offers a smooth and comfortable ride whether you’re commuting to work, running errands, or enjoying a relaxed weekend cycle. The upright riding position provides better visibility and reduced strain, making longer journeys feel effortless.

Specifications

Bike Type Hybrid / City Bike
