Ride Through History

Join us on 23rd January for a journey through 40 years of cycling memories!

From rusty first rides and weekend repairs to long commutes, club runs, and lifelong friendships, this special event celebrates four decades of Alf’s Cycles at the heart of our community.

Step inside our story through photographs, classic bikes, and shared memories from riders past and present. Meet fellow cyclists, swap stories, enjoy light refreshments, and relive the moments that made Alf’s more than just a bike shop — a place where a love for cycling was passed on, one ride at a time.

Whether you’ve been riding with us for years or are discovering Alf’s for the first time, come celebrate the passion, people, and journeys that shaped 40 unforgettable years.
