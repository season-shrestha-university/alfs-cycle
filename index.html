Keeping Cheriton cycling for generations

Alf’s Cycles has been a friendly, family-run bike shop at the heart of our community. From children’s bikes to commuter and hobbyist cycles, we offer honest advice, expert repairs, and a personal touch that keeps customers coming back for generations.

Pop in to see our carefully chosen bikes, accessories, and safety gear — or just for a chat about your next ride. At Alf’s, you’re always part of the family.
